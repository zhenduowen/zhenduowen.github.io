Let $X$ and $Y$ are random variables (or $Y$ is a $\sigma$-field), we can have that

$$
E_X(X) = E_Y(E_X(X|Y))
$$

$$
Var_X(X) = E_Y(Var_X(X|Y)) + Var_Y(E_X(X|Y))
$$
