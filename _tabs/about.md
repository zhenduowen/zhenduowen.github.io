I grew up in a Sinopec oilfield community in [Henan, Puyang](https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Puyang), a cozy little city on the north shore of the Yellow River. My hometown features four distinct seasons, with a golden autumn.

I am currently a second-year PhD student in the Department of Information Engineering at CUHK, where I have the privilege to be advised by Prof. Amin Gohari. Prior to this, I earned my master's degree from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego, where I worked with Prof. Alon Orlitsky. Before that, I graduated from the Department of Statistics at CUHK-Shenzhen with first-class honors in 2022.

 My research interests lie in information theory and learning theory. In general, I enjoy working with abstract yet transferable concepts derived from practical problems.
